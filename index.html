Early Blight

94% Confidence
Symptoms
Dark brown spots with concentric rings, yellow halos around lesions, typically starting on lower leaves.
Treatment
Apply copper-based fungicides every 7-10 days. Remove infected leaves and improve air circulation.
Prevention
Rotate crops annually, avoid overhead watering, ensure proper spacing between plants.
